Formula used:
Damage factor is proportional to:
1/3 × Ainf × √finf + Acav × √fcav + 4/3 × Aarc × √farc × 1.1

A is the attack factor for that troop type, based on the leader’s attack %, lethality %, and selected troop-specific hero modifier. The 1.1 on Archery is because Bear troops are Infantry.
