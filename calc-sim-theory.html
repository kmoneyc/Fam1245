Theory
Important: These tools are inspired by public Frakinator-style features, but they do not copy private app code or claim to reproduce hidden Kingshot combat exactly. They use transparent formulas that are useful for comparing formations and decisions.
ToolWhat it optimizesFormula ideaBest use
Bear Hunt SimulatorDamage pressureTroops × Attack × Lethality × Bear modifierFind high-archer damage ratios from your own battle-report stats.
Mystic Trial SimulatorNo-hero troop mixAttack × Lethality × Health × counter matrixEstimate best Infantry/Cavalry/Archer split vs a visible enemy mix.
Battle SimulatorMarch comparisonStats × hero/buff modifier × troop counter coefficient × capacity factorCompare two setups and see which change moves the result.
How to make the math useful: Use battle-report stats, not just base Town Center stats. Run a real test, enter the report numbers, then adjust enemy stats until the result roughly matches. After calibration, the tool is much better for testing “what if I change ratio/heroes/buffs?”
